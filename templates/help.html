Using the Gym Clan Gym Management System

Viewing All Members

  1. Access the Members Menu on the Main Navigation

View More Details about a Member

  1. Access the Members Menu on the Main Navigation
  2. Click See More Link on the left of the member details.

Find a members Session History

  1. Access the Members Menu on the Main Navigation
  2. Click See More Link on the left of the member details.
  3. Click on the Session History button at the bottom of the screen.

Edit Member Details including changing Memeber Status and Member Type

  1. Access the Members Menu on the Main Navigation
  2. Click See More Link on the left of the member details.
  3. Click on the Edit Memeber button at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Make required changes.
  5. Click Save.

Delete a Memeber

  1. Access the Members Menu on the Main Navigation
  2. Click See More Link on the left of the member details.
  3. Click on the Delete button at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Click Delete to confirm the deletion.

How to Add a Member

  1. Access the Members Menu on the Main Navigation
  2. Click on the Add a Member button at the bottom of the page.
  3. Complete the new member details.
  4. Click Save

Viewing All Sessions

  1. Access the Sessions Menu

View More Details about a Session

  1. Access the Session Menu on the Main Navigation
  2. Click See More Link on the left of the session details.

Find members Booked onto a Session

  1. Access the Session Menu on the Main Navigation
  2. Click See More Link on the left of the session details.
  3. Click on the Bookings button at the bottom of the screen.

Cancel a Member off of a Session (from Session)

  1. Access the Sessions Menu on the Main Navigation
  2. Click See More Link on the left of the session details.
  3. Click on the Bookings button at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Click on the Cancel Button on the left of the member detail.
  5. Click OK on the Confirmation Page to return to the sessions list.

Edit Session Details

  1. Access the Session Menu on the Main Navigation
  2. Click See More Link on the left of the session details.
  3. Click on the Edit Session button at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Make required changes.
  5. Click Save.

Delete a Session

  1. Access the Session Menu on the Main Navigation
  2. Click See More Link on the left of the session details.
  3. Click on the Delete button at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Click Delete to confirm the deletion.

How to Add a Session

  1. Access the Session Menu on the Main Navigation
  2. Click on the Add a Session button at the bottom of the page.
  3. Complete the new session details.
  4. Click Save

How To Add a Booking

  1. Access the Members or the Session Menu on the Main Navigation
  2. Click on the Add a Booking button.
  3. Choose the relevant member and relevant gymsession from the dropdown lists.
  4. Click Save to confirm the booking.
